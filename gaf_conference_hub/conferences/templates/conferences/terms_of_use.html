Terms of Use

By using this platform, users agree to provide accurate information and to use the system only for legitimate conference activities.

Author responsibilities

  • Authors must ensure that all listed co-authors have approved the submission.
  • Uploaded papers must be in DOC or DOCX format and smaller than 50 MB.
  • Submitted content must comply with academic integrity and publication ethics standards.

Reviewer responsibilities

  • Reviewers must keep manuscript files confidential.
  • Reviewers must declare any conflict of interest.
  • Reviewer comments should be professional, constructive and respectful.

System use

Users must not attempt to access files, pages or data for which they do not have authorization.

Changes

The conference team may update these terms as necessary for conference management and compliance.
