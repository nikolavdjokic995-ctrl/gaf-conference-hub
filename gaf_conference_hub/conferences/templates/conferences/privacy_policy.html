Privacy Policy

This platform is used for managing submissions, peer review, conference communication and publication workflow for Green Building International Scientific Conference.

Data we collect

  • Account information such as name, email, affiliation, title and country.
  • Submission metadata such as paper title, abstract, keywords, authors, co-authors, countries and emails.
  • Uploaded manuscript files and review-related files.
  • Reviewer assignments, review reports, editorial decisions and email logs.

How data is used

Data is used only for conference administration, peer review, communication with authors/reviewers and preparation of accepted papers for publication.

File access

Manuscript files are available only to authorized conference roles according to the workflow: authors, judges, assigned reviewers, layout reviewers and managers.

Retention

Temporary files such as anonymized reviewer files and reviewer-uploaded commented files may be deleted automatically after final acceptance to preserve storage and protect confidentiality.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact the conference editorial office.
